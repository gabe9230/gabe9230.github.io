About Me

Hi, I’m Gabriel, and I’m passionate about bringing ideas to life through thoughtful design, problem-solving, and hands-on execution. I thrive at the intersection of creativity and technical precision, and I’m always looking for new ways to push boundaries while keeping practicality at the core of everything I build. Whether I’m working on a complex project or a simple concept, I enjoy digging deep, learning fast, and delivering results that are both innovative and reliable.

My background spans engineering, machining, and software design, I take pride in being self-taught in many areas while constantly expanding my skill set. I believe that real growth comes from curiosity, persistence, and a willingness to take on challenges outside of my comfort zone. Over the years, I've worked on a program that converts JS to Gcode, a 3d Printed glider, and multiple games and simulations. Each one sharpening my eye for detail and my ability to adapt under pressure.

Outside of work, I’m someone who’s always learning—whether it’s mastering a new tool, studying languages, or exploring new ideas in science, technology, and design. I value meaningful collaboration, clear communication, and a mindset focused on building things that last. If you're interested in working together or just want to connect, feel free to reach out!
